Welcome to Tsehaye Mehari Traditional Women's Clothing Store

Tsehaye Mehari is a passionate artist whose journey in fashion began in 2003, inspired by a love for handwoven textiles and Ethiopian cultural roots. Over the years, he has developed a reputation for designing Habesha dresses that beautifully combine authenticity with modern style. His designs celebrate the beauty of Ethiopian craftsmanship while offering fresh, innovative interpretations for the modern wearer. Dedicated to quality and creativity, Tsehaye continues to influence the next generation of Habesha fashion.

Tsehaye Mehari creates all types of Habesha dresses including wedding kemis, modern styles, cultural outfits, and everyday simple wear.

About the Designer - Tsehaye Mehari

Who is Tsehaye Mehari?

Tsehaye Mehari is a skilled Ethiopian fashion designer who has spent more than two decades perfecting his craft. Born with an eye for detail and a talent for creativity, he began designing professionally and quickly became known for his clean stitching, unique patterns, and high-quality finishing.

He specializes in creating all types of Habesha clothing, including modern kemis, cultural dresses, wedding gowns, and simple everyday wear. His work is deeply connected to Ethiopian identity, and he takes pride in designing pieces that make women feel elegant, confident, and connected to their heritage. With a strong commitment to craftsmanship and customer satisfaction, Tsehaye continues to grow his brand and serve as an inspiring figure in the Habesha fashion industry.
